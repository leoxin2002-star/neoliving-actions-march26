  • No live work without prior approval and risk assessment

Project Information
Scope of Work
  • Confirm crane access route to site
    Check overhead power lines, road width, ground conditions
  • Site setup — establish crane pad and outrigger positions
    Ground must be firm and level — use crane mats if required
  • Pre-lift safety briefing with all personnel
    All crew to attend — sign attendance sheet
  • Inspect rigging gear — slings, shackles, spreader bars
  • Module 1 lift and placement
    Confirm orientation and exact landing position
  • Module 2 lift and placement
  • Module 3 lift and placement
  • Module 4 lift and placement
  • Verify all modules aligned and level
  • Temporary bracing / securing of modules
  • Pack down crane and clear site
Special Instructions
Safety Requirements
  • Lift plan must be reviewed and signed off before any lifting
  • Exclusion zone must be established — no personnel under suspended loads
  • Wind speed limits: STOP lifting if wind exceeds 40 km/h (or as per lift plan)
  • Licensed crane operator and rigger required at all times
  • PPE required: hard hat, hi-vis vest, safety boots, gloves, safety glasses
  • Spotter/dogman required for all lift operations
  • Ensure overhead power lines identified — maintain safe distances
  • Emergency plan briefed to all personnel before commencing

    Photograph condition before loading

    Chains, straps, and edge protectors as required

    Coordinate with crane operator for timing

Special Instructions
Safety Requirements
  • All loads must comply with NHVR load restraint guidelines
  • Driver must hold appropriate heavy vehicle licence
  • Over-size / over-mass permits arranged prior to transport (if required)
  • Escort vehicles required for wide loads as per permit conditions
  • PPE required at factory and site: hard hat, hi-vis vest, safety boots
  • No reversing on-site without a spotter
  • Report any damage or incidents immediately to Neo Living contact
